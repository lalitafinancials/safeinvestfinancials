The Problem with Traditional Career Paths

Most people follow the same script: go to school, get a job, work for 40 years, retire on whatever's left. And for decades, that script worked well enough. But today's workers are waking up to a difficult truth:

  • Your salary is someone else's decision — no matter how hard you work
  • Your promotions depend on your boss's approval, office politics, and company performance
  • If you train someone better than you, they become your competition — or your replacement
  • You build someone else's company, someone else's equity, someone else's dream

There's nothing wrong with a W-2 career. But if you've ever felt the ceiling above you — the invisible limit on what you can earn, what you can become, what you can build — you understand exactly why more people are looking for a different path.

What Does the Income Actually Look Like?

Let's be honest — income in this business is entirely performance-based. There's no salary. What there is, is unlimited upside. Here's a realistic picture based on different levels of effort:

⚠️ Honesty First — This Is Not for Everyone

This is a commission-based business that requires consistent effort, resilience, and a genuine desire to help people. There's no guaranteed paycheck. The first 3-6 months are the hardest as you build your client base and team. If you're looking for a quick scheme or passive income without work — this isn't it. If you're willing to put in the effort, the upside is extraordinary.

How Do You Get Started? 4 Simple Steps.

1

Schedule a Discovery Call with Lalita

No pressure. No pitch. Just a 30-minute conversation where we learn about your goals and share exactly how the business works. You ask every question you have. We'll be honest if it's not the right fit.

2

Get Licensed (We Guide You Every Step)

You'll need a state insurance license to sell products. Most people complete their licensing in 2-4 weeks. We provide study resources, guidance on the exam, and support throughout the process. The cost is modest — typically $100-$200 for exam fees.

3

Join the HGI Platform & Start Training

Once licensed, you gain access to HGI's Aristotle management system, weekly training calls, product knowledge resources, and direct mentorship from our team. Your first goal is your first 3 clients — we'll work alongside you to get there.

4

Build, Grow, and Lead Your Own Team

As you gain confidence and production, you'll start building your own team — recruiting and training others the same way you were. Every person you bring in multiplies your impact and your income. This is where the business really begins.

💡 The HGI Difference — You Train Your Team, Not Your Competition

In traditional business, training someone better than you means creating your own competition. In HGI's model, you train your team — and their success directly increases your income through override commissions. You are incentivized to help the people around you succeed. That changes everything about the culture.
